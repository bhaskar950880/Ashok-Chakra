5. Eligibility

The Ashok Chakra can be awarded to:

•Officers and enlisted personnel of the armed forces.

•Members of paramilitary forces.

•Civilians who display extraordinary bravery or self-sacrifice.



6. Selection Process

•Nominations are made by the respective military or civil authorities.

•A high-level committee evaluates the nominations.

•The award is presented by the President of India during the Republic Day Parade on 26th January.

7. Notable Recipients

Some famous recipients of the Ashok Chakra include:
